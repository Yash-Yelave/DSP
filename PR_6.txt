Aim :- Design a Digital IIB Butterworth filter with given specifications.
%Title: Design a Digital IIR Butterworth Filter with given Specifications
clc; clear all; close all;
%% Filter Specifications

Fp	=	20; % Passband Frequency in Hertz
Fs	=	600; % Stopband Frequency in Hertz
Ap	=	4; % Passband Attenuation in dB
As	=	17; % Stopband Attenuation in dB
SR	=	7000; % Sampling Rate in Samples/Sec
ftype = 'low'; % Filter Type Wp = 2*Fp/SR;
Wp = 2*Fp/SR
Ws = 2*Fs/SR; % Passband & Stopband Frequencies (pi not reqd)
%% Design required Filter [n,Wn] = buttord(Wp,Ws,Ap,As);
[n,Wn] = buttord(Wp,Ws,Ap,As);
[b,a] = butter(n,Wn,ftype);
%% Plot its Frequency Responce freqz(b,a);
freqz(b,a);
title('Frequency Resonce');
